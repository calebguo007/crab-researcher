# CrabRes: Global Vibe Coder Persona & Activation Strategy

> **Date**: 2026-04-02
> **Author**: 老产品 (Product Growth & Consumer Psychology Expert)

## 1. User Personas: Domestic vs. International

### A. The "Global Vibe Voyager" (International)
- **Profile**: Solo founders, non-technical creators, or senior devs tired of corporate life. Often based in US/Europe/India.
- **Mindset**: "Build in Public" is a lifestyle. They value the *creative process* and *shipping speed*. They want to be seen as innovative and fast.
- **Pain Point**: Shipping 5 products but getting 0 users for all of them. "I'm a great builder, but marketing feels like a chore."
- **Psychological Trigger**: **Empowerment & Status**.
- **Preferred Channels**: X (Twitter), Reddit, Indie Hackers, YouTube.

### B. The "CN Money-Maker Coder" (Domestic)
- **Profile**: Chinese developers, often with "Big Tech" background, facing age/career anxiety. Highly focused on *monetization* and *going overseas (出海)*.
- **Mindset**: "Code is a tool for income." They are pragmatic and look for the "shortcut" to USD revenue. "Vibe coding" is a means to achieve the "One-person company" dream.
- **Pain Point**: "How do I sell to users in the US/Europe when I don't understand their culture?" or "My app is great, but how do I get it in front of English-speaking users?"
- **Psychological Trigger**: **Loss Aversion (Missing the wave) & Economic Security**.
- **Preferred Channels**: Xiaohongshu, Jike, WeChat, X (Overseas focus).

## 2. "Aha Moments" & Activation Strategies

### Strategy A: The "Speed-to-Market" Hook (Global)
- **Aha Moment**: Within 2 minutes of inputting a URL, CrabRes shows a **90-day "Copy-Paste" Growth Roadmap** that looks like it was written by a pro marketing agency.
- **Activation Script**:
    > "You've done the hard part—you built it. 🦀 Now, let my 13 experts find your first 1,000 users while you sleep. No templates. No generic advice. Just 90 days of actionable, copy-paste ready tasks."
- **心理钩子**: 利用“赋能效应”。让用户觉得营销不再是黑盒，而是可以像部署代码一样清晰。

### Strategy B: The "Dollar-First" Hook (Domestic)
- **Aha Moment**: CrabRes performs a **"Global Market Gap Analysis"**, showing the user exactly which Reddit communities are complaining about their competitors' high prices or missing features.
- **Activation Script**:
    > "代码已经商品化了，增长才是你的护城河。🦀 别在红海里内卷，我的专家团帮你挖掘海外市场的真实需求。直接给你写好能发在 Reddit 和 X 上的英文文案，帮你赚到第一个 $1,000。"
- **心理钩子**: 利用“损失厌恶”。如果不使用 CrabRes，你就在错过全球市场的红利；反之，它是你降维打击、赚取美金的最强外挂。
