## 阶段二：渠道扩张与 KOL 杠杆 (第 31-60 天)

**核心逻辑**：利用 **权威效应 (Authority)**，通过垂直领域博主的背书快速破圈。

### 针对 Voyager (全球/海外)

I'd love for your audience to see a 'live growth research' session. Can we set up a trial for your community? 🦀"
